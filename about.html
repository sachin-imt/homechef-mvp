About HomeChef Sydney

We connect busy people with talented home cooks in their neighborhood. Real food, grown locally, cooked authentically.

The Problem

  • Too expensive to eat out at restaurants every single day.
  • Too exhausted to cook nutritious meals after a long workday.
  • Traditional food delivery apps are often unhealthy, highly processed, and create massive plastic waste.

The Solution

Subscribe to a local chef's home-cooked meals for the week.

Authentic recipes passed down through generations. Real ingredients. Fair prices that support local families rather than massive tech corporations.

Our Story

Started in 2025 in the heart of the Inner West.

We saw thousands of incredible home recipes being kept within families, while neighbors resorted to expensive, unhealthy takeout. We built HomeChef to bridge that gap.

Currently serving: Newtown, Marrickville, and Erskineville.
