How CelebChef Works

Choose your journey.

1. Find Your Chef

Browse local passionate home chefs and culinary talent. From heritage family recipes to authentic homestyle masters, find a chef whose menu matches your cravings and dietary needs.

2. Subscribe Weekly

Choose your starting week and subscribe. No lock-in contracts. You'll receive a curated menu of 5 restaurant-quality meals delivered every week.

3. Gather & Enjoy

Your chef prepares your meals fresh daily. Delivered straight to your door in eco-friendly containers, simply sit down and savor the food together with family or friends. Experience elevated dining without the prep.
