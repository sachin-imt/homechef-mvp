Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a subscription?

Yes, but it is week-by-week. You are never locked in, and you can cancel anytime before the new week begins.

Can I choose specific dishes?

No, you get the chef's full 5-meal set curated for that week. This allows our chefs to focus on quality and reduce waste.

What if I don't like the food?

We offer a First-Week Money-Back Guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your experience, we'll refund your first week.

How is this different from apps?

This is real home cooking. You're supporting local cooks directly, eating healthier, less processed food than standard fast-food delivery.

What areas do you deliver to?

Currently, we exclusively serve Newtown, Marrickville, and Erskineville. We will be expanding to all of Inner West Sydney soon.

How do I pay?

Currently via a secure Bank Transfer. We email you the instructions upon subscribing. Credit card processing (Stripe) is coming soon.
